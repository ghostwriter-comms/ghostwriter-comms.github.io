Support

Quick answers to the things people ask most. Stuck on something not listed here? Email ghostwriter.comms@gmail.com.

Recording on iPhone

Tap the record button on the main screen. Ghostwriter will request microphone access the first time. Tap pause to interrupt without losing context; tap stop when you’re done. The recording opens straight to the transcript view.

Recording on Apple Watch

Install the GhostwriterWatch companion app from your iPhone’s Watch app. The first time you record from the Watch, iOS will ask for HealthKit permission — this is required to keep the recorder running with the screen off (see Privacy). Recordings transfer to your iPhone automatically when you stop.

Transcription models

Settings → Transcription Model lets you pick between Whisper variants:

  • base.en — default. Fast, runs on every supported device. Good for clear single-speaker audio.
  • small.en — better accuracy with a moderate speed cost.
  • large-v3-turbo — highest accuracy. Heavier; best on newer devices.

Models download once and run entirely on-device thereafter.

BYOK Gemini summarization

Settings → Summarization lets you supply your own Google Gemini API key. When enabled, summaries are generated by Google’s cloud instead of the on-device MLX model. The transcript is sent directly from your device to Google’s API; Ghostwriter is not a relay. Disabling this and removing the key restores fully on-device behavior.

Troubleshooting

Watch app won’t start recording

Open the iPhone’s Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Ghostwriter and confirm Workouts is enabled. Without it, iOS will not grant the background-execution permission the Watch app needs.

Transcription model download fails

Models are downloaded from Hugging Face. Make sure you have a stable Wi-Fi connection and a few hundred megabytes free. Pull-to-refresh on the model picker to retry.

Transcript has repeated lines

Whisper occasionally repeats phrases on quiet or ambiguous audio. Ghostwriter strips most of these automatically. If you see persistent runs of duplicates, email the recording (or a description) and we’ll tune the filter.
