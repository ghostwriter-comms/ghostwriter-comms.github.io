About Ghostwriter

Ghostwriter is an indie iOS app for capturing the meetings you actually need to remember — without sending a single second of audio to a server you don’t control.

Most meeting assistants are built around a backend. They have to be: cloud transcription is the easy path. The trade-off is that every word you say lives on someone else’s machine, gets indexed, gets retained, gets used to train the next version of whatever.

Ghostwriter takes the harder path. Transcription runs on WhisperKit, Apple-Neural-Engine-targeted Whisper inference. Summarization runs on MLX, on-device LLMs from Apple’s research team. The Apple Watch companion is a real Watch app, not a remote control. Everything works offline.

If you want cloud-quality summaries, you can plug in your own Google Gemini key and the request goes straight from your device to Google — Ghostwriter is never in the middle.

Built independently, with care. Questions, bug reports, or just notes from a fellow privacy nerd are welcome at ghostwriter.comms@gmail.com.
