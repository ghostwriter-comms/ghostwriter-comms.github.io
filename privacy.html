Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-23

Ghostwriter is designed so that your meeting audio, transcripts, and summaries never leave your device unless you explicitly opt in to cloud features with your own API key.

What we collect

Nothing. Ghostwriter does not have a server. There is no account system, no telemetry, no analytics SDK, and no third-party tracking. We have no way to know who uses the app or how.

What stays on your device

Audio recordings, transcripts, summaries, and your settings are stored locally on your iPhone or iPad. They are included in your iCloud device backup if you have one enabled, the same way any other app’s files are. Apple, not Ghostwriter, manages that backup.

Bring-your-own-key cloud (optional)

Ghostwriter offers an optional summarization mode that uses Google’s Gemini API. This feature is off by default. If you choose to enable it:

  • You provide your own Gemini API key, stored in the iOS Keychain on your device.
  • When you tap “Summarize,” the transcript text is sent directly from your device to Google’s API.
  • Ghostwriter is not a relay. We do not see, log, or store the transcript or the response.
  • Google’s handling of that request is governed by Google’s own privacy terms.

HealthKit

The Apple Watch companion requests a HealthKit workout session purely to keep the audio recorder running while the screen is off. No health or fitness data is read or written. No workout is saved to the Health app.

Children

Ghostwriter is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a substantive way, the “Last updated” date above will change and the updated version will be posted here.

Your Responsibility as a User

You are responsible for the content of your recordings and for complying with applicable laws in the jurisdictions where you record. Recording laws vary by location — in some places, all parties to a conversation must be notified or must consent before being recorded. Ghostwriter does not monitor, review, or process your recordings; how you use the app is entirely up to you.
