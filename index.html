Meeting notes that never leave your device.

Ghostwriter transcribes and summarizes your meetings entirely on-device. No accounts. No servers. No analytics.

Download on the App Store

On-device by default

Transcription runs on WhisperKit. Summarization runs on MLX. Your audio never touches a server you don’t control.

Apple Watch companion

Start a recording from your wrist. Pause and resume across iPhone and Watch. Segments stitch together automatically.

Bring-your-own-key cloud (optional)

Prefer cloud quality? Plug in your own Gemini API key. Traffic goes from your device to Google directly — we’re not in the loop.

Privacy by architecture

No analytics SDK. No third-party trackers. The privacy nutrition label says “Data Not Collected” because that’s the truth.
